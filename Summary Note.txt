Master data
1/ REQ Attributes (10): reference (ID), urgency, priority, author, ownership, complexity, source, status, Risk, Stability
2/ Phân tích CR cần lưu ý các yếu tố nào: cost (Effort), benefit, course of action, priority, risk
3/ Planning approach: adaptive, predictive
4/ Format of document: formal, informal, presentation
5/ Platform communication: group, individual, email & non-verbal
6/ Elicitation type: experiment, research, collaborative
7/ REQ model format: metric, diagram
8/ REQ category: people and role, activity flow, data & information, rationale, capability
9/ REQ characteristics: atomic, concise, consistent, prioritized, understandable, testable, unambiguous, feasible, and complete.
10/ Solution approach: create, purchase, combine
11/ opp: increase efficiencies (Eliminate redundancy), improve access to information, and identify additional capabilities.
12/ chọn design option: resources, constraint, dependencies between REQ.
13/ Các loại Trace Relationship: Derive, Depends (Necessity/Effort), Satisfy, Validate
14/ Các tiêu chí Prioritization: Benefit, Penalty, Cost, Risk, Dependencies, Time Sensitivity, Stability, Regulatory or Policy Compliance.
15/ Khi xảy ra CR thì cần phân tích những phần sau: Benefit, Cost, impact, Schedule, urgency.
16/ Risk Tolerance: Risk-aversion, Neutrality, Risk-seeking.
17/ Khi collect performance measure thì cần lưu ý: Volume or Sample Size, Frequency and Timing, Currency.
